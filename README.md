# National Unity and Prosperity Act (NUPA) Framework

**The first complete private-sector post-scarcity economic operating system.**

Authored solely by Brandon Anthony Bedard between November 18, 2025 and February 26, 2026.

NUPA is a finished, ready-to-implement framework that solves multiple major structural problems simultaneously — at **zero taxpayer cost**:

- Settles the 160-year 1865 Freedmen debt through private-sector revenue instead of appropriations
- Restores genuine economic sovereignty to Tribal Nations
- Creates a market-based Fixed Cost Arbitrage firewall that makes human labor fiscally cheaper than automation
- Converts 24 million acres of dormant BLM land into self-funding Community Land Trusts

### Core Architecture
- **Perpetual Sovereignty Licensing (FASL)**: Irrevocable licensing of underutilized BLM land into Community Land Trusts with Tribal strategic veto and Fifth Amendment protection.
- **40/40/20 Recursive Reinvestment Model**: 40% Sovereign Royalty to Tribe, 40% Beneficiary Dividend to verified descendants, 20% reinvested into infrastructure and knowledge.
- **Fixed Cost Arbitrage**: Dramatically lowers fixed land costs for corporations that prioritize human labor.
- **VQPV Governance**: Balanced four-part board (Tribal, Beneficiary, Local Official, Fiduciary).
- **Self-Dissolving Agency**: NUPALTA sunsets once all boards are seated.
