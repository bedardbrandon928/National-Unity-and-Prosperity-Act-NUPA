# THE SOVEREIGN COVENANT: NUPA-LR (LIBERIAN ADAPTIVE)  

**I. Institutional Governance: The Communal Key**  

 * **Weighted Authority:** Every Community Land Trust (CLT) is governed by a Variable Quad-Partite Vetting (**VQPV-LR**) Board.
 * **The Sovereign Seat:** The relevant Customary Leadership, Traditional Council, or Community Board of Trustees holds a permanent, non-expiring seat on this board.
 * **Mandatory Consent:** No commercial development or 99-year ground lease may be executed without the express, written consent of the Customary Community as formalized under the Land Rights Act of 2018.
 * **The Anti-Leakage Guardrail:** Any administrative or external political action that attempts to marginalize community authority or bypass the VQPV-LR board triggers a Mandatory 10-Day Resolution Window. If the breach (such as administrative infringement or attempted fund diversion) remains unresolved at the conclusion of Day 10, it triggers an **Automatic Protective Dissolution** of the CLT charter, effectively freezing all corporate utility and activity within the hub until local sovereignty is restored.

**II. Asset Economic Structure: The 80/20 Sovereign Adaptive Waterfall**  

This revenue is derived from the 99-year Ground Leases and Annual Ground Rent paid by global corporate tenants.
 * **80% Ground Rent Dividend (Local Community Land Trust):** Flows directly to the local CLT as an unrestricted payment for communal dividends, local healthcare, and education endowments. This ensures that the primary value generated by the land stays immediately and tangibly with the people who live on it, delivering real growth compared to the historical baseline.
 * **20% Recursive Reinvestment (The Hardware Fund):** Hard-coded funding for on-site infrastructure maintenance. This fund self-funds Tier-4 industrial infrastructure like independent power grids, roads, dedicated fiber optics, and vocational training, ensuring the hub is a self-sustaining asset at zero cost to the taxpayer and available to protect the local workforce.

**III. Legal Protections: The Vested Licensing Protocol (VLP)**

 * **Irrevocable Finality:** The activation of customary land via the Land Rights Act of 2018 operates as an irrevocable license to utilize the asset for private industrial development to permanently discharge historical stagnation and infrastructure deficits.  
 * **The Statutory Shield:** Once activated via a **Deterministic Vesting Handshake**, the asset and its economic interests are categorized as **Vested Property Rights**. Under Liberian law, these rights are protected from arbitrary revocation or bureaucratic seizure.  
 * **Liability Allocation:** Any attempt by central administrative or external predatory interests to reverse this activation or "recapture" the non-federal yield (the 80% Community share) is legally categorized as an **"Administrative Breach of Sovereign Trust."** This makes the offending entity or administrative office solely liable for immediate legal liability for community-led economic damages—including the full projected value of 99 years of ground rent.

**The Architect's Tactical Update:**  

The preservation of the **80/20 Sovereign Adaptive Waterfall** for Liberia is a deliberate architectural decision designed to completely bypass central bureaucratic leakage. While other jurisdictions use a split-stakeholder framework to manage different systemic risks, NUPA-LR anchors itself directly onto the statutory hardware of the **Land Rights Act of 2018**, making the customary community the supreme sovereign actor. By hard-coding 80% of the gross ground rent to the people and 20% back into the hardware, the system is fully insulated. If external predatory interests try to interfere, the **Limited Veto Provision** and immediate liability protocols shut them out completely, preserving the hub as a true "Human Sanctuary."
