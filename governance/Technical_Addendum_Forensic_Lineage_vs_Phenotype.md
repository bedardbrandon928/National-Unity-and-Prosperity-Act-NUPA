- **Debt Assignment:** A debt owed to an ancestor is a property right that passes to the heirs. The federal government’s failure to deliver the "40 acres" was a default on a specific group of individuals.  
- **Private Fiduciary Action:** NUPALTA, acting as a non-federal agency, is not "granting a preference"; it is settling a claim.  
- **Universal Standing:** Because the DNA markers associated with the Freedmen can appear in any American regardless of their current racial "label," the program is universal in its forensic application, even if it is specific in its historical target.
