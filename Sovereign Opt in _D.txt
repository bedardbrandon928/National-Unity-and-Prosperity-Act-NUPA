MEMORANDUM OF SOVEREIGN UNDERSTANDING & COOPERATIVE ALLIANCE OPT-IN

INSTRUMENT: Sovereign Consent & Pre-Enactment Strategic Alliance

PARTIES:
THE SOVEREIGN NATION: The [NAME OF TRIBE] (hereinafter "The Nation").
THE TRUST AUTHORITY: The NUPA Land Trust Authority (Pre-Chartered Entity), represented by its Provisional Administrator (hereinafter "The Authority").

ARTICLE I: THE SOVEREIGN CONSENT (THE "GREEN LIGHT")

1.1 Formal Opt-In

Pursuant to the "Sovereign Opt-In Requirement" of the NUPA framework, The Nation hereby formally grants its written consent to participate in the Cooperative Alliance.

1.2 Authorization to Proceed

The Nation authorizes The Architect and NUPALTA to identify, survey, and secure "Option Agreements" with corporate partners for specific tracts of underutilized federal land adjacent to or within The Nation’s ancestral territory, no less than, but not to exceed, 8,000 acres, specifically for the purpose of establishing NUPA Community Land Trusts (CLTs).  

ARTICLE II: THE RESTORATION OF LAND (THE ASSET)

2.1 The Licensing

The Parties acknowledge that the core mechanism of this Alliance is the congressional authorization of the licensing of federal assets to the Trust.

2.2 Perpetual Sovereign Licensing

The Nation supports the Perpetual Sovereignty Licensing Protocol, understanding that once license of the title passes from the Federal Government to the CLT, it shall be privately and irrevocably held, protected by the Fifth Amendment against future reclamation.

2.3 No Threat to Existing Reservation

This Agreement applies strictly to external federal lands licensed to the Trust and does not affect the sovereign status of existing Reservation land.  

ARTICLE III: SHARED GOVERNANCE (THE VQPV)

3.1 The 25/25/25/25 Mandate

The Nation acknowledges that the CLTs shall be governed by the Variable Quad-Partite Vetting (VQPV) System.

3.2 The Sovereign Seat

The Nation shall maintain a permanent, distinct, and equal voting seat on the VQPV Board ("Tribal Sovereignty"), ensuring that no land use decision can be made without Tribal oversight.  

3.3 Balance of Authority

 * Mutual Veto Power: The Nation acknowledges that while it possesses the ultimate authority to approve or deny leases (The Strategic Veto), this power is balanced by the fiduciary and regulatory duties of the other VQPV members.  
 * Stewardship Accountability: The Nation may not overrule documented safety, environmental, or labor-protection "Red Flags" raised by the Fiduciary, the Local Official, or verified Beneficiaries.  
 * System Integrity: This balance ensures the Hub remains a Tier-1 bondable asset and prevents any single party from incurring liability for the collective Trust.  

ARTICLE IV: ECONOMIC BENEFITS & REVENUE SHARING

4.1 Revenue Sharing

In exchange for this Alliance, The Nation shall receive a guaranteed, proportional share of all commercial lease revenue (e.g., from solar farms, data centers) generated by the CLTs, as defined in the final Charter.

4.2 Corporate Option Fees

The Nation acknowledges that "Pre-Ignition" Option Fees paid by corporate partners shall be utilized immediately to fund the administrative startup of NUPALTA and the surveying of these lands. 

ARTICLE V: EXECUTION

5.1 Non-Binding on Assets

This Memorandum does not encumber Tribal assets or funds. It is an agreement to utilize restored federal assets for commercial use.

5.2 Immediate Effect

Upon signature, The Authority is authorized to present this Memorandum to the U.S. Congress and Corporate Partners as proof of the "Cooperative Alliance," satisfying the conditions for the sale of Pre-Ignition Options.

ARTICLE VI: DEFENSE AGAINST OVERREACH

6.1 Sovereign Trigger

The Nation maintains the right to declare a "Breach of Sovereign Trust" if Federal agents attempt to bypass the VQPV board or audit private Framework Usage Fees.  

6.2 Protective Dissolution

A declaration of a "Breach of Sovereign Trust" initiates a 10-day Countdown for Remediation. During this window, all commercial activity continues under "Strict Sovereign Observation." If the government fails to provide a verifiable retraction of the overreach within these 10 days, the Indivisibility Mandate fully executes, dissolving the CLT and shifting all resulting commercial damage liabilities exclusively to the offending party's office.

6.3 Government Accountability

The Parties agree that the financial repercussions of such a dissolution—specifically the loss of corporate revenue and the cost of litigation—fall exclusively upon the offending party's office, as a functionary of the U.S. Government, shielding The Sovereign Nation and the Architect from liability.
